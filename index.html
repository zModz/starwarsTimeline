c. 300 BBY

Comic

Star Wars: The High Republic, is an ongoing multimedia project primarily featuring work from writers: Cavan Scott, Claudia Gray, Charles Soule, Daniel José Older, and Justina Ireland. It spans many different storytelling formats, including comics, novels and audio plays, to tell one cohesive overall story set in the High Republic Era, two centuries prior to the events of Star Wars: Episode I The Phantom Menace. Soule has described The High Republic project as a "mega-story."
